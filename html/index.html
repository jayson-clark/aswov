Who we are

We are local to
your community

We are locally operated by dedicated residents right here in the Ohio Valley and all donations to our chapter go directly to granting the wishes of our local children battling life-threatening disorders.

We proudly serve the local children in Belmont, Brooke, Guernsey, Hancock, Harrison, Jefferson, Marshall, Monroe, and Ohio Counties in the states of Ohio and West Virginia.

About A Special Wish

The only major wish granting organization in the US that grants wishes to qualifying infants, children, and adolescents from birth up to the age of 21.

Our Chapter, the
Ohio Valley Chapter  ASWOV

We proudly opened our doors in 1992. In the last 30 years we have had the opportunity to grant over 150 wishes to local children right here in the Ohio Valley. We are your neighors, friends, and family and we are dedicated to serving the children of our communities. We are so grateful and thankful to all of our supporters who have helped us grant these special wishes!

Individual donations are still our largest single source of funding, making a direct impact to granting wishes

We are locally operated and funded entirely through the generosity of Ohio Valley residents, businesses, and organizations. No matter how big or small, we treat every donation with extreme financial care. Without you none of this would be possible.

All "Special" Wishes begin with a referral. Anyone can refer a child for consideration to A Special Wish and we will contact the family. Brothers, sisters, neighbors, nurses, doctors, coaches, family and friends can help be part of something special!

Volunteers are the backbone of our organization

Assisting in achieving our mission to grant as many wishes as we can for children with life-threatening illnesses in the Ohio Valley. All of our volunteers are local Ohio Valley residents. Donate just by doing your shopping online. You shop. Amazon Gives.

AmazonSmile is a way customers can support their favorite charitable organization every time they shop with Amazon, at no additional cost. Amazon will donate 0.5% of the price of your eligible purchases to help us grant special wishes. For instructions click below.

AmazonSmile Instructions

AmazonSmile Instructions

1. Open the Amazon Shopping app
2. Navigate to the main menu (=)
3. Tap on Settings and then select “AmazonSmile”
4. Select your charity and then follow the on-screen instructions to turn ON AmazonSmile in the mobile app
5. Once AmazonSmile has been activated in your app, future eligible app purchases will generate a donation for the charity you have selected
